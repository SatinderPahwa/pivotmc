Paul's frustration was familiar to any operations manager: a capable team, strong customer demand, modern equipment — and yet production targets consistently fell short.

The culprit was an assembly line chokepoint that slowed the entire production process. His production manager Sarah had attempted various solutions — streamlined workflows, machinery optimisation, additional shifts — yet the bottleneck persisted when processing certain orders.

When "everything seems fine" but performance still lags, traditional Lean tools can only take you so far. TAKT time reveals *where* the constraint lives. TRIZ reveals *why* it persists — and how to dissolve it rather than work around it.

The combination of these two methodologies cuts through the noise — and the chokepoints — to uncover root causes of inefficiency that neither approach would find alone.
